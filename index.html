3DSciSolutions

3DSciSolutions, aims to use human cells to grow fully functional lungs in which disease states or conditions can be replicated. A myriad of different drugs and treatments can then be tested to see what works for a particular disease and can be used to develop personalised medical treatments. Our company is made up of a diverse, multilingual team of researchers with a passion for ethical research.

Lung Disease

Lung diseases such as asthma, lung cancer, pneumonia, tuberculosis, cystic fibrosis and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) are an important global health problem (Medical Line Plus, 2019).

Hundreds of millions of people worldwide suffer from lung-related diseases. Asthma kills around 1000 people every day and affects as many as 339 million people worldwide. As many as 470,000 people in Ireland have asthma. COPD is now the fourth largest killer in the world and lung cancer is the most common cancer in men and women around the world (American Lung Association, 2019).
Here in Ireland we have one of the highest levels of cystic fibrosis in the world and is therefore often referred to as the Celtic Curse.


People affected by lung diseases usually suffer from them for the rest of their lives and their quality of life substantially lower than that of a healthy person. Therefore, it is of the utmost importance that new therapies are developed in an ethical, efficient and effective manner.
