Our Product

We believe our product will help to confront these two major issues. We want to use human skin cells to grow fully functional lungs in which disease states can be replicated. We would then be able to understand the pathological progression of a disease as well as study the effectiveness of drugs.

We chose lungs as our first product because as I mentioned earlier, lung disease affects hundreds of millions of people worldwide. There are approximately 300 million people suffering with asthma worldwide with at least 450,000 here in Ireland. COPD is the fourth largest killer worldwide with an estimated 4 million deaths a year. Lung cancer is now the most lethal cancer, 2500 people are diagnosed with lung cancer each year in Ireland. Cystic fibrosis has a particularly high prevalence in Ireland and is often fittingly referred to the Celtic Curse.

We believe that our product is the best option because our lungs will be more physiologically similar to human lungs than mice and this will give rise to more accurate results.
